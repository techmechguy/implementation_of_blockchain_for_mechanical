About Our project

No more tax invasion

Our GST chain system to avoid tax evasion starts from the point when the customer would pay a bill which would have GST details to the merchant. Once when the transaction is in processing state our system would take GST amount from the invoice and would directly transfer it to the government bank account and the rest of the amount would be transferred to the merchant account no. Which would be registered on our app with a unique business ID.Now what if the merchant is not registered ?

In this scenario,our transaction would fail and our system would send a message to both consumer and merchant of transaction unsuccessful.So the solution of the problem is that the merchant must be registered on our app.now here comes the role of our admin who would take various information from the merchant like name, gender, aadhar card,registered business ID etc and verify it and would send an email to the merchant about the confirmation of registration.The admin would also take care of verification of all transaction as well as the queries regarding it. Now,after the merchant is also registered and then the consumer pays the bill ,the transaction would be successful and the message regarding it would be send to the merchant as well as the customer.
